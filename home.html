CSE

Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is an exciting and in-demand field in engineering. It provides a broad range of fundamental and advanced concepts for students.

  • Programming Languages
  • DSA
  • DBMS
  • Computer Networks
  • Operating Systems
  • Data Science
  • Web Development
ECE

Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) program offers students to study a combination of electrical engineering and computer science topics.

  • Circuit Analysis and Design
  • Electronics
  • Signals and Systems
  • Digital Logic and Design
  • Microcontrollers and Microprocessors
  • VLSI Design
MECH

Mechanical Engineering (MechE) program offers students to study a diverse range of topics related to the principles and applications of mechanical systems.

  • Statics and Dynamics
  • Mechanics of Materials
  • DBMS
  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics
  • Heat Transfer
  • Heat Transfer
